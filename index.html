About Christina

A decade dedicated to deeper human connection

Christina Weber is a social entrepreneur, ICF-certified coach, and a leading authority in the relationship industry with more than a decade of experience helping individuals, couples, and professionals forge meaningful connections.

Originally from Annapolis, Maryland, Christina has lived in Los Angeles, New York City, and Bali before settling in Austin, Texas. Her journey began in 2014 hosting "Underground Unattached" in NYC, which led to founding WeDeepen.

Through innovative concepts like "Shop & Shag" at Erewhon (featured on the cover of LA Magazine and the Bill Maher Show), Christina has redefined what it means to bring people together.
