The Experience

A transformational weekend
for the love of your life.

Love Immersion is a 3-day live retreat designed to break through the patterns keeping you stuck in love — and to install the embodied skills, language, and presence that extraordinary relationships require.

It's part workshop, part homecoming, part rewiring. Led by Christina Weber and a rotating team of world-class love guides, every Immersion is intimate, deeply human, and built around real breakthroughs — not theory.

Whether you're single, partnered, or somewhere in between — you'll leave changed.

The Framework

The Four Pillars

01

Self-Intimacy

Come home to yourself. You cannot give what you have not first become.

02

Presence

The rare, rewiring gift of being fully met — and fully meeting another.

03

Communication

The embodied language of honest desire, boundary, and repair.

04

Commitment

The daily practice of choosing — and being chosen — on purpose.
